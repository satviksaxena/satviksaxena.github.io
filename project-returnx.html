ReturnX

Make a Repair request on our Portal and get a Price and Delivery Time Quote

About

This is a Spring Boot Microservice based project.

It consist of 5 Microservices

Name Deployed At About
1 Portal https://returnxportal.herokuapp.com/ Frontend Main portal of app
2 Authorization https://returnxauth.herokuapp.com/ Provides JWT Authorization to each Microservice
3 Component https://returnxcomponent.herokuapp.com/ Performs Calculations and contacts Packaging and Payment MS
4 Packaging https://returnxpackaging.herokuapp.com/ Provides Delivery date and Packaging Charges
4 Payment https://returnxpayment.herokuapp.com/ Provides Payment Confirmation
